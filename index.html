scruffian

def: a scruffy ruffian

I make things. I particularly like to make music. Some of my music is on bandcamp. There is more on last.fm.

I play for automattic.com. My career history is on Linked In.

I put photos on my photo blog and words on my WordPress. Sometimes I work on my javascript bible study tool.

Music is meant to be fun, that's why it's 'played'. I love to play music with or for anyone, I am just waiting for someone to ask.

This album was recorded between 2010 and 2015 in the shed by the woods at Woodstock Lodge and various other locations.
