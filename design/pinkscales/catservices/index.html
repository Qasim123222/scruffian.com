David Coulommiers - Programmer/writer/producer

David is a highly innovative programmer and has worked extensively in this area, contributing to works by a broad range of artists including Elodie Fregé (UniversalMercury), Star Academy France, Shaggy, Ste McNally, BBMAK etc. His studio houses an impressive programming/recording rig along with David's vintage synths which include Fender Rhodes, MiniMoogs , MS20, TB303,VCS-3 Putney,CS-80 etc which he uses to create his innovative sound. He is highly skilled with all todays sampling technology, of which he also has a vast library. Sound Design, incorporating Symphonic Orchestral works show his amazing talents to their full - David has a complete PRO VSL station and also uses Miroslave Vitous and EWQL Symphonic Orchestra. Established in his native France as a gifted writer, he has worked on many sountrack projects, including work for Buena Vista (ABC, Disney) showcasing his signature orchestral/string arrangements. During the last year he has contributed to several 'number one' selling albums and is now concentrating on production and artist development.
