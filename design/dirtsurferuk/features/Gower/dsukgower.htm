the gower

 190502: The Gower peninsular is the best place for surfing in Wales. Around the beach lie large sand dunes which looked like they would make great riding, with chutes, drop off and loads of other possibilities. During the day the sand was very soft because it was walked on, so we tried it earlier in the morning, hoping that they might be wetter and firmer. They weren't. After many failed attempts and a few good ones, we moved up to the hill that overshadowed us.  
   
 
It was very big and we were too scared to go to the top (it it a lot bigger than it looks in the photos). This terrain was good for Will, who is just learning to ride, except that the landings (although softened by sheep poo) were much harder. The ride down was still fairly challenging as it was really rocky and the path had large sections cut away. After a series of good runs and busted knees we had a few more ideas on how to ride the dunes, and got back down to the beach with a body board.
