street
not content with all of these possibilities, dirtsurfers have been know to stray into previously uncharted territory. why build your own jumps when someone else has built skate parks for you - don't let the kids on their crappy skateboards put you off. cities are also filled with steps, benches and loads of other stuff which looks like it was made for surfing - and a multistory car park run has to be done.
