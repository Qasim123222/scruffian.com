22-23 june 2002: atb.mag are hosting the first ever dirtsurfer event in the UK. It is being held at Wiscombe downhill, Devon. Matt fro the dirtsurfer team will be there along with loads of other UK riders. There will be dirtsurfers available to borrow for anyone who wants to, but you have to let J at atb.mag know.
There are some hair-pin bends, so a working brake is highly recommended. You will also need pads to take part. For any other info email J.
This page will be updated when/if we have more details.
