kent
p.o.r.c. - penshurst contains a duel course with a big start ramp and a series of large tabletops and a few doubles, a large quarry with big jumps and step ups and lots of fast steep slopes for downhilling. (designed for bikes) We've never actually taken dirtsurfers there (only bikes) so we are not sure if you will have clearance problems with the front end on the jumps. Don't forget you have to pay. jumps and singletrack confident - advanced streetmap
 
hemstead forest has a range of single track and wide forest roads. from the main car park go off to your left (as you enter) and follow this path round to the right to get to the steepest bit of downhill singletrack/fire roads competent streetmap
 
mote park - maidstone mainly very shallow, but nicely smooth, grassy slopes, not very fast. There are a few steep chutes grass beginner - novice streetmap
 
bewl water - lamberhurst this the main reservoir for kent and has a few shallow slopes near the car park/campsite and some steeper slopes which run into the water! ideal for beginners and for getting the board seen as their are always a lot of people around. grass beginner - competent streetmap
surrey
XBP - nutfiled this centre opened in june and is has slalom, jumps border cross and a snowboad style half pipe. want more details? grass and jump all levels streetmap
berkshire
the look out - bracknell filled with tracks of all type from wide, shallow slopes to twisty, fast singletrack to steep downhills with chutes and jumps. singletrack/fire roads competent - advanced streetmap
